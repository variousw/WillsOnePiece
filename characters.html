Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Star Rating: 5/5

Things I Like About Them:

Luffy is my favorite One Peice Charchter. He does what he thinks is right no matter what, is brave, positive, funny, and isnt even afraid of death. Luffy is someone I strive to be like, and someone I wish i can be like when achicving goals of my own. Luffy's journey of becoming Pirate King and liberating people of the sea is very inspiring and similar to what I want to do in my life.

Favorite Moment:

So far Luffys best moment has to be when he hit the bell to save the Skypieans and Shandoreans from Eniru, it showed Luffy's selflessness and heroicism.

Things I Like About Them:

Zoro's dedcation to Luffy and to becoming the geatest swordman is beyond inspiring. His raw strength and stoiscm makes him one of my favorite starw hats.

Favorite Moment:

Either when he took all of Luffy's pain in Thriller Bark or when he chopped through Mr. 1's metal in thier fight, or Zoro's fight agaonst Monet. All of these show how strong Zoro is and is just really cool uses of his strength and power.

Jimbei

Star Rating: 4.5/5

Things I Like About Them:

Jimbei is very strong and humble, with honor that could match anyone. His loyalty to Luffy and lifelong dream to change the world is very admirable along with his goal for fishman equality. Jimbei expresses his love for the crew with complete respect and action to defend the Straw Hat Pirates goals.

Favorite Moment:

When He shared Blood with Luffy. It showed his dedication to Luffy, and presented his belief of equality for Fishmen and humans, acting as a rolemodle to both groups.
